Description

This is a game of Evil Hangman! In this game, the computer cheats by waiting until the last moment to actually pick a value for the word. Every time you guess a letter, it places the letter (or chooses not to place the letter) where the most words in it's dictionary has it. Then, it narrows down it's dictionary based on the letter position and has you guess again, repeating the process. It has three difficulty levels, which makes the computer alternate between picking the largest and second largest list at set intervals. Have fun and good luck!
